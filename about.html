About The Lemon

Here at The Lemon we try to write by our motto: Bright Ideas, Sour Critics. We try to challenge whatever it is we are critiquing, expand on whatever issue we’re discussing, and enlighten our readers by providing quality content sure to deter any mental scurvy. We’re sour but not tart. That is to say we don’t write with bitter language. Instead we try to keep it fresh but still with the tang of critical examination and analysis. Whether it be commentary on the gaming industry, political satire, or a deep dive into society’s mental health issues, The Lemon’s purpose is to leave a sour taste such that one’s mental salivary glands are thoroughly activated and you leave us with nothing but the urge for more.
