Cindy Karina Cano

She also enjoys nature photography and poetry, which she posts on her Instagram.

Victor Hernandez
Contributor

Victor Hernandez has been a fan of professional Wrestling since he was shot out of the womb. The moment he first opened his eyes he saw WWF champion Bret Hart on the hospital room TV. The doctor spanked his bottom, and Victor gave doc a right hand to the jaw. From WWE to NJPW, Victor LOVES the sport of professional wrestling. He may not know all 7 continents, but he knows all 78 Intercontinental champions in WWE history and all 10 in NJPW history (which is just as impressive). If you ever want to discuss pro wrestling, hit him up on Twitter and Facebook.



Amie Faye
Contributor

Half bookworm, half butterly, Amie Faye's a student at the University of San Francisco married to writing (and slightly married to Justin Bieber). She has been writing since the young age of eight. Her first story she ever wrote was about a bat that got wings by helping with the groceries. But over the years, her writing has gone through metamorphosis like herself. Besides writing, she also adores book thrifting, dogs, wandering, photography, concerts, and laughter.

Social media: @aamiefayee on Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat.
