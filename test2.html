  • Amie Faye
    Contributor

    Half bookworm, half butterly, Amie Faye's a student at the University of San Francisco married to writing (and slightly married to Justin Bieber). She has been writing since the young age of eight. Her first story she ever wrote was about a bat that got wings by helping with the groceries. But over the years, her writing has gone through metamorphosis like herself. Besides writing, she also adores book thrifting, dogs, wandering, photography, concerts, and laughter.

    Social media: @aamiefayee on Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat.
